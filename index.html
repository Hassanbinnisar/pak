Pakistani Dishes

Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish made with meat (usually chicken or mutton), rice, and spices such as saffron, cumin, and cinnamon. It is often served with yogurt or raita and is a favorite during special occasions.

Chicken Karahi

Chicken Karahi is a spicy and savory curry made in a wok-like vessel called 'karahi'. The dish is prepared using chicken, tomatoes, ginger, green chilies, and a variety of spices. It's best served with naan or rice.

Haleem

Haleem is a slow-cooked dish made with a combination of meat, wheat, lentils, and spices. It's a thick and creamy dish often garnished with fried onions, coriander, and a squeeze of lemon juice.

Nihari

Nihari is a rich, flavorful stew made with slow-cooked beef or mutton and aromatic spices. It is typically enjoyed as a breakfast dish, paired with naan or paratha.

Seekh Kebab

Seekh Kebab consists of minced meat, herbs, and spices, which are formed into skewers and grilled to perfection. It's a popular street food and often served with naan and salad.

Chapli Kebab

Chapli Kebab is a spicy and aromatic minced meat patty, typically made with beef or mutton, herbs, and spices. It's fried until crispy and served with naan or rice.

Saag

Saag is a hearty dish made from mustard greens, often cooked with spices, and served with makai ki roti (cornflat bread). It's a popular dish in the rural parts of Punjab.

Aloo Keema

Aloo Keema is a flavorful dish made with minced meat (usually beef or mutton) and potatoes. It's a simple yet hearty dish, often enjoyed with naan or rice.

Bhindi Masala

Bhindi Masala is a popular vegetarian dish made with okra (bhindi) cooked with onions, tomatoes, and spices. It's commonly served with roti or rice.

Chana Chaat

Chana Chaat is a refreshing snack made with boiled chickpeas, onions, tomatoes, cucumbers, and tamarind sauce, garnished with fresh coriander and spices.

Samosa

Samosas are deep-fried pastry pockets filled with spiced potatoes, peas, or minced meat. They are a popular snack in Pakistan, often served with chutney.

Pakoras

Pakoras are savory fritters made by deep-frying vegetables, meat, or paneer coated in a spiced gram flour batter. They're commonly served as a snack during rainy weather.

Shahi Tukda

Shahi Tukda is a royal dessert made with fried bread soaked in milk, flavored with saffron, and topped with nuts. It is often served at weddings or special occasions.

Kheer

Kheer is a rice pudding made with rice, milk, sugar, and cardamom, typically garnished with nuts and raisins. It's a popular dessert during festivals.

Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a sweet dessert made from deep-fried dough balls soaked in sugary syrup flavored with rosewater or cardamom. It is a favorite at celebrations.

Jalebi

Jalebi is a sweet, deep-fried dessert made from coiled batter soaked in sugar syrup. It's crispy on the outside and soft inside, often enjoyed with milk.

Halwa Puri

Halwa Puri is a traditional breakfast dish in Pakistan, featuring sweet semolina halwa served with deep-fried puris and a side of chickpea curry.

Chicken Tikka

Chicken Tikka consists of boneless pieces of chicken marinated in spices and yogurt, grilled to perfection. It's commonly served with naan or as part of a barbecue platter.

Fish Curry

Fish Curry is a flavorful curry made with fish, tomatoes, and spices such as turmeric and garam masala. It is often served with rice or naan.

Kadhi Pakora

Kadhi Pakora is a savory yogurt-based curry served with fried gram flour dumplings (pakoras). It's typically enjoyed with rice.

Pulao

Pulao is a rice dish cooked with spices and often meat or vegetables. It’s a simpler alternative to biryani, typically served with yogurt or raita.

Khichdi

Khichdi is a comfort food made with rice and lentils, cooked together with spices. It's nutritious and easy to digest, often eaten when feeling under the weather.

Ras Malai

Ras Malai is a sweet dessert made from soft cheese patties soaked in sweetened milk. It is a popular dish during special occasions and festivals.

Lassi

Lassi is a refreshing yogurt-based drink, which can be served sweet or salty. It’s perfect for cooling down in the hot summer months.

Payaa

Payaa is a slow-cooked stew made from beef or mutton trotters, flavored with aromatic spices. It is often enjoyed as a comforting winter meal.

Zarda

Zarda is a sweet dish made from rice, sugar, and flavored with cardamom, saffron, and dry fruits. It's a popular dessert, especially during celebrations.

Beef Stew

Beef stew is a hearty, slow-cooked dish made with chunks of beef, potatoes, carrots, and a variety of aromatic spices. It's a comforting dish, best served with rice or bread.

Sagwan

Sagwan is a traditional dish made from spinach cooked with spices, offering a rich flavor. It's typically served with roti or rice.

Gosht

Gosht refers to a variety of meat dishes, typically lamb or mutton, cooked with spices. It's a flavorful curry best enjoyed with naan or rice.

Dahi Puri

Dahi Puri is a snack made with crispy puris filled with yogurt, tamarind chutney, and spices. It's a popular item in Pakistani street food culture.

Mutton Makhni

Mutton Makhni is a creamy and rich curry made with mutton cooked in a tomato-based gravy and a variety of spices. It's usually served with naan or rice.

Saad Keema

Saad Keema is a flavorful minced meat curry cooked with ginger, garlic, tomatoes, and an assortment of spices, served with naan or rice.

Chana Dal

Chana Dal is a traditional dish made from split chickpeas cooked with spices. It's a hearty, protein-packed vegetarian dish served with roti or rice.

Lahori Chana

Lahori Chana is a spicy and tangy chickpea curry cooked with onions tomatoes, and various spices. It's best enjoyed with naan or paratha.
